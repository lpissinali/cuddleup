What is CuddleUp?

CuddleUp is a cuddling for hire service. Through our platform, CuddleUp connects users with a match of their choice and lets them experience a sense of enlightenment and well being through cuddling. Cuddle at the movies, on a beach, or simply hug it out in the comfort of your home! CuddleUp lets you enjoy the emotional rewards of a cuddle!

For Cuddlees

Simply set up a profile on CuddleUp and you will be ready to browse through our listing of available local Cuddlers. Once you find someone that you would like to hire for a cuddling session, simply contact him or her through CuddleUp to arrange a time, place, location, and price.

For Cuddlers

Looking to make a bit more money or promote your current cuddling services? CuddleUp makes it easy to list your services, find, and connect with people looking to hire a cuddler. Using CuddleUp is free and makes it easy to list and market your services.
